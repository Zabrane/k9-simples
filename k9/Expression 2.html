10 Expression Evaluation

k9 has a compact way to evalue an expression on a data structure including dictionaries and tables. 

Expr :a+b

 d:`a`b`c!(3 2 1;1 2 3;1 2 3);d
a|3 2 1
b|1 2 3
c|1 2 3

 d :a+b*c
4 6 10

t:+d;t
a b c
- - -
3 1 1
2 2 2
1 3 3

 t :(a+b*c)
4 6 10
