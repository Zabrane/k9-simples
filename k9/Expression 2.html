10 Expression Evaluation

k9 has a compact way to evalue an expression on data structures including dictionaries and tables. 

Expr :a+b

10.1 expr ⇒ x :y

Evaluate expression(s) y on table or dictionary x. The space between x and : is required otherwise it becomes set. 

 d:`city!`London
 d :city
`London

 d :`Paris=city
0b

 d:`city!`London`Paris`Madrid
 d :`Paris=city
010b

 d:`a`b`c!(3 2 1;1 2 3;1 2 3);d
a|3 2 1
b|1 2 3
c|1 2 3

 d :a+b*c
4 6 10

t:+d;t
a b c
- - -
3 1 1
2 2 2
1 3 3

 t :(a+b*c)
4 6 10

 d:`a`b`c!(3 2 1;1 2 3;1 2 3);d
 d :(100*a;b+c)
300 200 100
2 4 6
