k9: Manual

The Shakti programming language is the work of Arthur Whitney and the team at Shakti. The language comes from a lineage of similar languages going back to APL. It’s also known as k9 given the similarties to k, k2, ..., k8 and the fact that Arthur also wrote those. k9 is still under development so expect a bit of syntax change and likley even more improvements.

This manual is for Shakti (k9) build 2020.09.29.
